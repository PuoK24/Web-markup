Koopvaarderplantsoen

Have you just moved to Amsterdam North and have a child/ren, and want to enjoy quality time with your family, Koopvaardersplantsoen, a child-friendly environment in our neighborhood is the ideal Destination. It is a safe and fantastic playground where children burn their energy and make new friends in the neighborhood. Family activities, barbecues, and playing facilities for the children, all are possible here, hopefully, while settling into your new home, you will enjoy them and have a great time with your loved ones. Koopvaardersplantsoen, as described by many as a “haven for family”, offers a range of opportunities to create cherished memories not to youngsters, but for adults as well. From North Station you can reach Koopvaarderplantsoen with GVB Bus 36 click here.
